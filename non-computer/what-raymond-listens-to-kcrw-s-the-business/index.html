What Raymond listens to: KCRW's The Business

KCRW’s The Business reveals how the sausage of the entertainment industry is made. Here are some of my favorites:

  • Bill Mechanic discusses the experience of producing the Oscar Awards, trying to make it more than just an award show, and failing.
  • Gregg Gelfand, associate director of American Idol describes what happens in the director’s booth during a live show and reveals a dirty little secret about the end-of-episode montage. (Related: Overheard in the Control Room.)
  • J.D. Shapiro and Corey Mandell, the two credited screenwriters for the movie Battlefield Earth (the worst picture of the decade), never met. Hear their story of how the project came to be, and how it went off the rails.
