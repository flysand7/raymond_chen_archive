Raymond misreads acronyms: MSPP-PVP

I was looking up some information about the Microsoft Partner Program, commonly abbreviated MSPP. I stumbled across an internal wiki called mspp-pvp. My first reaction upon seeing the name was, “What, it’s player versus player?” I got this images of the CEOs of the various partners wearing VR goggles and running around playing laser tag.

No, it’s the Partner Velocity Program.
