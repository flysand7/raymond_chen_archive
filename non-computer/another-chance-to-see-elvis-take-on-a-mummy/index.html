Another chance to see Elvis take on a mummy

For those in the Seattle area who missed it last year, The Fremont Outdoor Cinema is screening Bubba Ho-Tep on Friday, July 9th. Go see it. You won’t be disappointed. Assuming what you were expecting to see was Elvis fighting a mummy. With the help of JFK. In a wheelchair.

If you were expecting something else, then okay maybe you’ll be disappointed after all.
